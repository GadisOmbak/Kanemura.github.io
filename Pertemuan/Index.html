OSUSHI

She can play an alto sax and recorder. She tried playing other instruments like guitar and ukulele but felt it was too hard for her to play, so after trying saxophone she decided to stick with it. She was featured in a pamphlet for her high school, holding a saxophone and advertising for the school's brass band club.

Her older brother is a big Nogizaka46 fan. She was influenced by him and she became a Keyakizaka46 fan after learning about them from Nogizaka46. She did ballet in the past. Her childhood dream was to become a flight attendant. She is known to be very hardworking. She has described herself as "tofu mental", a term for people who are pessimistic or mentally anxious. It is associated with people who are perfectionist in nature, but criticize themselves when things go wrong. The term refers to the way tofu can fall apart easily.

In 2018, her personal goal was to be more self-confident and positive. She ranked fourth in the first Hinatazaka46 Academic Ranking test on Hiragana Oshi. She then she earned the title of "Smarty Queen" during the buzzer round. She usually gets mistaken for being older than her age (mistaken for being a university student) by store clerks, etc. She can do a Stitch (Lilo & Stitch) voice impression. She is a fan of the anime and manga series "Attack on Titan". Her favorite character is Sasha.

She is close friends with Ushio Sarina. On a segment on HINABINGO!, chose Sarina as the ideal big sister and Sarina chose her as the ideal little sister. The two have even formed an unofficial duo called Daikanyama Sisters. She is close friends with Kosaka Nao, as they are of the same generation and only three days apart in age. Their duo name is called NaoMiku. The pair performed the duet See Through for Hinatazaka46's first album.

When she was chosen as the center of Hinatazaka46's 6th Single, Nao sent her a text saying, "If it's you, you can do it". She, Nibu Akari, and Watanabe Miho are known as the "Saitama Trio", as all three members are of the same generation and grew up in Saitama Prefecture. They sometimes carpool home together. The three formed the unit Color Chart together for the song Akubi Letter on Hinatazaka46's 6th Single Tteka.
